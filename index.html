PiratesGuide 2019:

The second edition of PiratesGuide, a preview of the 2019 Pittsburgh Pirates written by online Pirates writers who focus on analytics. PiratesGuide 2018:

A look into previewing the 2018 Pittsburgh Pirates with analytical pieces. I helped coauthor a chapter about the Pirates and their outfield defense using visualiations and batted ball data. Along with two other pieces about the 2018 Pirates.
